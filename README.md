# FUTURE_PE_01  
## Task 1: AI Website Copy Generator for Local Businesses  

---

## 1. Objective  
To build a structured AI prompt system that generates professional website copy for a local beauty salon including homepage content, service descriptions, and strong call-to-action sections.

---

## 2. Tools Used  
- ChatGPT  
- GitHub  

---

## 3. Business Example Chosen  

Beauty Salon Name: Glow & Grace Beauty Salon  
Location: Bangalore  
Target Customers: Women aged 18–45  
Services: Hair styling, bridal makeup, facials, skincare treatments  
Goal: Increase walk-in customers and online appointment bookings  

---

## 4. Final Prompt Used  

Act as a professional website copywriter.

Generate website copy for a beauty salon with the following details:

Business Name: Glow & Grace Beauty Salon  
Location: Bangalore  
Target Audience: Women aged 18–45  
Services: Hair styling, bridal makeup, facials, skincare treatments  

Requirements:
1. Write an engaging homepage headline and introduction.
2. Write service page descriptions for each service.
3. Include emotional appeal and customer benefits.
4. Add a strong call-to-action encouraging bookings.
5. Keep tone elegant, warm, and professional.
6. Make it suitable for a modern salon website.

---

## 5. Generated Output  

### Homepage Headline:
“Glow With Confidence, Shine With Grace”

### Homepage Introduction:
Welcome to Glow & Grace Beauty Salon, Bangalore’s trusted destination for premium beauty and skincare services. We believe every woman deserves to feel confident, radiant, and beautiful. Our expert stylists and skincare specialists provide personalized treatments designed to enhance your natural glow.

---

### Our Services

*Hair Styling:*  
From trendy haircuts to smoothening and styling, our professionals create looks that match your personality and lifestyle.

*Bridal Makeup:*  
Look flawless on your special day with our professional bridal makeup services designed to enhance your natural beauty and last all day.

*Facial Treatments:*  
Rejuvenate your skin with customized facials that cleanse, hydrate, and restore your natural glow.

*Skincare Treatments:*  
Advanced skincare solutions tailored to your skin type to help you achieve healthy and radiant skin.

---

### Call to Action:
Book your appointment today and let Glow & Grace bring out the best version of you. Call now or schedule online for a luxurious beauty experience.

---

## 6. Prompt Structure Explanation  

The prompt clearly defines:
- The role (professional website copywriter)
- Business details
- Target audience
- Required outputs (homepage, services, CTA)
- Tone and writing style

This structure ensures the AI generates focused, relevant, and conversion-driven website content.

---

## 7. Key Learnings  

- Structured prompts produce better and more specific results.
- Mentioning role and tone improves output quality.
- Clear business details help AI generate realistic content.
- CTA placement is important for conversions.
